About Wylde Jewellers - Bristol

Located at 6 The Mall, Clifton, Wylde Jewellers - Bristol has been a beacon of exceptional craftsmanship and personal service for generations. As a premium Goldsmith, we pride ourselves on creating heirloom-quality pieces that capture moments and tell stories.

Our dedicated Expert Team brings passion and precision to every design, repair, and bespoke creation. From initial concept to final polish, we ensure meticulous attention to detail, upholding our Quality Guaranteed promise.

We believe in building lasting relationships with our clients, offering a warm and professional experience tailored to your unique needs. Come and discover the difference of truly bespoke British jewellery.
